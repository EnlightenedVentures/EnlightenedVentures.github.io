Privacy Policy

Effective date: November 3, 2025

Overview. UmpClicker is designed to run entirely on your device and does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to Enlightened Ventures LLC.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or contact lists
  • No precise location data
  • No financial or payment information
  • No health or sensitive data
  • No analytics or advertising identifiers

App Functionality

UmpClicker maintains simple counters during your session. Data resets on app close or reset actions and is not transmitted off‑device.

Third‑Party Services

UmpClicker does not integrate third‑party SDKs for analytics or advertising. If this changes, this policy will be updated before any new version is released.
